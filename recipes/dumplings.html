Dumplings

These Ukrainian halfmoon-shaped stuffed dumplings can be sweet or savory, served boiled or fried, garnished with fried onions and served with sour cream, or served plain.

Ingridients

  • Dough
    • water
    • all-purpose flour
    • salt
    • an egg or a table soon of oil
  • Filling
    • mashed potatoes
    • fried onion

Steps

  1. Combine the egg with water and salt, and whisk to break it up.
  2. Gradually incorporate the flour and knead the resulting dough until it no longer sticks to your hands
  3. Mix the filling
  4. roll the dough into big sheets and punch out the circles with an upturned glass or biscuit cutter.
  5. Cook in boiling water until varenyky start to float
