Onion Soup

Easy to make but delicious sweet soup with onions, cheese and toasted bread

Ingridients

  • 6 big onions
  • 4 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • salt
  • 8 cups of stock
  • toast
  • 1,5 cups grated cheese
  1. peel and slice onions
  2. caramelize teh onion with oil and butter
  3. add the stock and spices
  4. cook for 30 more minutes
  5. toast the bread
  6. put bread on the soup, add cheese and send to the oven until the cheese melts
